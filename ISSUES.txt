Known issues of this release (0.4.0):

 - FFmpeg/FLAC will only work if you ship it with your game.
   Steam or Chrome FFmpeg may be loaded, but it cannot be used by blitwizard.

 - Physics support is incomplete and doesn't support hinges/skeletons yet.

 - 3d is mentioned everywhere, but it's not available yet.

 - The Android port is in a pretty incomplete and not really usable state.
